MCMV Climate Resilience Tracker

Minha Casa, Minha Vida (MCMV - My House, My Life) is a nationally subsidized affordable housing program in Brazil.

Reference: 30 September 2024  ·  Source: Ministry of Cities  ·  This dashboard is developed by WashU PHP Unit

This dashboard visualizes Minha Casa, Minha Vida (MCMV) Faixa 1 projects (2009-2019) across Brazil, combining project-level data from the Ministry of Cities with a Climate Resilience Index (CRI) computed using climate exposure, sociodemographic, and built environment indicators. Use the filters below to explore projects by region, state, city type, financial agent, and year. The map, charts, and table below update together to reveal spatial patterns of affordable housing production and the climate resilience.

Citation: Wang, Y., Hockman, E., Augusto de Paula da Silva, A., Favarão Leão, A. L., Franco Silva, M., Nascimento Neto, P., Florindo, A., & Reis, R. (2026). Geocoded Dataset of Brazil’s Minha Casa, Minha Vida (MCMV) Faixa 1 Projects, 2009-2019 [Dataset]. Harvard Dataverse. https://doi.org/10.7910/DVN/B3HNWS
